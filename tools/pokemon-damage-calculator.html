About the Pokemon Damage Calculator

This calculator helps trainers predict the damage output of Pokemon moves during battles. By considering key battle factors, you can make strategic decisions about move selection and team composition.

How to Use:

  • Enter your Pokemon's level and attack stat
  • Input the move's base power
  • Provide the opponent's defense stat
  • Include any relevant modifiers (type effectiveness, STAB, etc.)

Key Features:

  • Accurate damage calculation using official game formulas
  • Considers all major battle factors
  • Helps optimize move selection strategies
  • Useful for competitive battle preparation
