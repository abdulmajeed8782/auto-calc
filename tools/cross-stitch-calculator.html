About the Cross Stitch Calculator

This calculator helps cross stitch enthusiasts determine the final dimensions of their projects based on stitch counts and fabric specifications. By inputting the number of stitches in both width and height directions, along with your fabric's thread count, you can quickly calculate the physical size of your finished piece.

How to Use:

1. Enter the number of stitches wide and high from your pattern
2. Input your fabric count (threads per inch/cm)
3. Select your preferred measurement unit (inches or centimeters)
4. Click Calculate to see your project dimensions

Why It's Useful:

Knowing your project's final size helps with:
- Selecting the right fabric size
- Planning framing requirements
- Estimating material needs
- Ensuring proper pattern scaling

The calculator automatically converts between inches and centimeters, and validates all inputs to ensure accurate calculations. Always add extra fabric around your design for framing or finishing purposes.
