About the Quadratic Formula Calculator

This calculator solves quadratic equations of the form ax² + bx + c = 0 using the quadratic formula. The quadratic formula (x = (-b ± √(b² - 4ac)) / (2a)) provides the roots of any quadratic equation, whether they are real or complex numbers.

How to use: Enter the coefficients a, b, and c from your quadratic equation. The calculator will instantly compute and display the roots. Ensure 'a' is not zero for valid quadratic equations.

Why it's useful: This tool eliminates manual calculation errors and quickly handles complex number calculations. It's ideal for students learning algebra, engineers solving real-world problems, and anyone needing to verify their quadratic equation solutions.
