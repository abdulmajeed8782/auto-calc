About Implicit Differentiation Calculator

This calculator helps find derivatives of implicitly defined equations using the implicit differentiation method. Unlike explicit functions where y is directly expressed in terms of x, implicit functions require special techniques to differentiate.

How to Use

1. Enter your equation in the form F(x,y) = 0 (e.g., x² + y² = 25)
2. Click Calculate to see the derivative dy/dx
3. Use Clear to reset inputs and results

Why It's Useful

Implicit differentiation is essential for solving derivatives of complex relationships that can't be easily solved for y. It's widely used in physics, engineering, and economics for analyzing interconnected variables.
