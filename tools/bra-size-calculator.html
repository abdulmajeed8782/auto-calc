About This Calculator

This bra size calculator helps you determine your correct bra size based on precise body measurements and converts between international sizing standards. Proper bra sizing is essential for comfort, support, and breast health.

How to Use:

  1. Measure your underbust (band size) in inches
  2. Measure your bust at the fullest point
  3. Enter measurements into the calculator
  4. Select your target location for size conversion

Why It Matters:

Wearing the correct bra size can prevent back pain, improve posture, and ensure proper support. International size conversions help when shopping for lingerie from global brands.
