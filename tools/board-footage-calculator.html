About Board Footage Calculator

The Board Footage Calculator helps you determine the volume of lumber in board feet, which is essential for woodworking projects, construction estimates, and material purchasing. Board feet is a standard measurement used in the lumber industry to quantify wood volume.

To use the calculator, simply enter the dimensions of your lumber in inches: thickness, width, and length. Then input the quantity of pieces. The calculator will automatically compute the total board footage using the industry-standard formula: (Thickness × Width × Length / 144) × Quantity.

This tool is particularly useful for carpenters, DIY enthusiasts, and construction professionals who need to accurately estimate material requirements and costs for their projects. It eliminates manual calculations and reduces the chance of measurement errors.
