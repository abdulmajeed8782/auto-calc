About the Theoretical Yield Calculator

This calculator helps determine the maximum amount of product (theoretical yield) that can be produced in a chemical reaction based on the stoichiometric relationships between reactants and products. It's essential for chemists and students to predict reaction outcomes and optimize experimental conditions.

How to Use

  1. Enter the mass of your limiting reagent in grams
  2. Input the molecular weight of the limiting reagent
  3. Provide the stoichiometric ratio from the balanced equation
  4. Enter the number of moles for the desired product
  5. Input the molecular weight of the desired product

Why It's Useful

Theoretical yield calculations help in:

  • Predicting reaction efficiency
  • Determining limiting reagents
  • Planning laboratory experiments
  • Calculating percent yield after experiments
