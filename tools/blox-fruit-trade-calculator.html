About This Calculator

The Blox Fruit Trade Calculator helps players evaluate the fairness of in-game item trades by comparing values adjusted for market demand. By selecting items from both sides of a proposed trade, the calculator automatically computes a Fairness Score to help determine if the exchange is equitable.

How to Use

  1. Check items you're offering in the left column
  2. Check items you're receiving in the right column
  3. Click Calculate to see trade valuation
  4. Compare total values and fairness score

Why It's Useful

This tool accounts for both base item values and current demand multipliers, providing a more accurate assessment than simple value comparisons. The Fairness Score helps prevent unfair trades by highlighting significant value discrepancies between offers.
