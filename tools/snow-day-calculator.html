About the Snow Day Calculator

This calculator helps predict the likelihood of school closures due to winter weather conditions. By analyzing key factors like snowfall amounts, temperature, wind chill, and your school district's historical closure patterns, it provides an estimated probability of a snow day.

How It Works

The algorithm considers multiple variables:

  • Snow accumulation: More than 6 inches significantly increases closure chances
  • Temperature: Sub-freezing temperatures help maintain dangerous conditions
  • Wind speed: High winds create dangerous wind chills and drifting snow
  • District policy: Some districts close more readily than others

Why It's Useful

This tool helps parents, students, and school staff prepare for potential closures by providing data-driven predictions. While not 100% accurate, it combines real-time weather data with historical patterns to give actionable insights.
