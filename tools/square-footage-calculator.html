About the Square Footage Calculator

This calculator helps you determine the area of various geometric shapes and includes unit conversion capabilities. Simply enter the required dimensions for your shape, select the appropriate units, and get instant area calculations. Key features include:

  • Supports rectangles, circles, triangles, and more
  • Automatic unit conversion between imperial and metric systems
  • Optional cost estimation based on quantity and price per unit

How to use: Enter the dimensions for your shape in any unit combination. For irregular shapes, use the appropriate combination of edges or bases. The calculator will automatically detect the shape based on your inputs and provide area calculations in multiple units.
