About the Aida Fabric Calculator

This calculator helps cross stitch enthusiasts determine the exact fabric size needed for their projects. By inputting your pattern's stitch counts and Aida cloth specifications, you can ensure you have enough fabric for framing and finishing.

How to Use

1. Enter your pattern's stitch dimensions
2. Specify desired border allowance in inches
3. Input your Aida cloth count (squares per inch)
4. Click calculate to get fabric dimensions

Why It's Useful

Prevents fabric shortages and ensures proper framing space. Accounts for common Aida cloth sizes and personal project preferences, making it essential for both beginners and experienced stitchers.
