About the Grade Calculator

This Grade Calculator helps students determine their current course standing and plan for final exams. By inputting assignment grades and their respective weights, users can calculate their current overall percentage in the course. The calculator also enables strategic exam preparation by showing the minimum final exam score needed to achieve a desired course grade.

To use the calculator: 1) Select your preferred input format (points, percentage, or letter grades), 2) Add all completed assignments with their weights, 3) Enter your final exam weight and target grade. The calculator will instantly show your current grade and the required final exam score to reach your target.

This tool is particularly useful for students who want to track their academic progress, identify areas needing improvement, and set realistic goals for final assessments. It eliminates guesswork in grade calculation and helps students make data-driven study decisions.
