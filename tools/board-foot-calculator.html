Board Feet:

About Board Foot Calculations

A board foot is a unit of measurement for lumber equal to 1 inch thick, 12 inches wide, and 12 inches long (144 cubic inches). This calculator helps you determine the total board feet for your lumber projects quickly and accurately.

How to Use:

  1. Enter the thickness in inches
  2. Input the width in inches
  3. Provide the length in inches
  4. Enter the number of pieces
  5. Click "Calculate" to get results

Why It's Useful:

Understanding board feet helps with material estimation, cost calculations, and project planning for woodworking and construction projects. This tool eliminates manual calculations and reduces errors in lumber quantity estimation.
