About This Calculator

This Cross Stitch Fabric Calculator helps crafters determine the exact fabric dimensions needed for their projects while accounting for necessary margins. By inputting your stitch count, fabric specifications, and desired border allowance, you can ensure you have enough material for framing and finishing.

How to Use

1. Enter your pattern's stitch dimensions
2. Specify your fabric count (squares per inch)
3. Add desired extra fabric allowance
4. Input the number of thread colors used
5. Click calculate to get precise measurements

Why It's Useful

Proper fabric calculation prevents material waste and ensures your design fits perfectly with adequate working space. The tool automatically adds border allowance to both sides of your design, following standard cross stitch best practices.
