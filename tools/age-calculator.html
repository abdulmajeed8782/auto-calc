About the Age Calculator

This Age Calculator helps you determine a person's exact age in years, months, and days. Simply enter the date of birth and the target date to calculate the precise duration between these two dates. This tool is particularly useful for:

  • Calculating age for medical purposes
  • Verifying age-related eligibility
  • Tracking developmental milestones
  • Historical age comparisons

The calculator accounts for leap years and varying month lengths to ensure accurate results. For best results, always use the calendar picker to select dates and ensure the target date is after the birth date.
